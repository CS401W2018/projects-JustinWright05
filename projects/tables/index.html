Facts about St. Chroma

  • St. Chroma is the masked character in Tyler, The Creator's new album Chromakopia.
  • St. Chroma is the awkward, shy, masked man in an army uniform that appears in Tyler's recent music videos.
  • St. Chroma's character is based off of Chroma the Great from The Phantom Tollbooth.
