Meet the Siblings

Gumball

Gumball is the oldest sibling of the Wattersons family. He is a blue cat and is a very funny person. One fun fact about Gumball Watterson is he can play the ukulele and sing very well.

Darwin

Darwin is the second oldest sibling in the family to Gumball. Darwin is a very affectionate person. As shown in many episodes he likes to hold hands and even likes to be kissed.

Anais

Anais is the youngest and most intelligent member of the Watterson family. Even though she is only four years old, she attends Elmore Junior High along with her brothers. Since she's the smartest in the family, she is constantly telling her brother Gumball what and what not to do.
