The Bonsai Tree

Bonsai

The Bonsai Tree is a miniature
tree. It is careful pruned, cared,
and shaped to create a naturally
beautiful tree shaped like a regular
sized tree. The Bonsai tree represents
a form of art and shows the harmony
of life, nature, patience, and balance.

The word Bonsai, comes from
the Japanese words bon which
means pot, and sai which means planting. The process of
creating a beautiful Bonsai tree is a living art form. The grower
of the tree expresses his patience, creativity, and understanding
of nature through his care of the Bonsai Tree.
