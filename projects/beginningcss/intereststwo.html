What Is Brawl Stars?

Brawl Stars is the greatest game of all time. The game is simple. You play as a character of your choice. Each character has a unique set of skills that sets it apart from another character. After you choose your character, you compete against other players in a battle to the death and fight until one person comes out on top.

My Favorite Character

I like a lot of characters in Brawl Stars but I gotta say my all time favorite character is Buzz. Buzz is a close range Brawler that breathes fire at the target. His super shoots a projectile at the opponent and stuns them leaving room for Buzz to do his thing. I love Buzz because of how beautful and cute he is. He makes me smile.
