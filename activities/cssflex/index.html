What you learned about Flex and CSS

Flex is one dimensional. Expands left to right, or top to bottom. It also allows items to wrap when you shrink the webpage.

Why do you need to have an accessible website? What does that mean?

Having access means that you can navigate through the website on all platforms. Mobile, and desktop. It's important because having an accesible website makes the people who go on the website have a better time navigating through it.
