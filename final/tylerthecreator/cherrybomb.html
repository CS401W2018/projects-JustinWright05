About Cherry Bomb

  • Released on April 13, 2015, as Tyler's fourth studio album.
  • It was released under Odd Future Records and Columbia Records.
  • The album blends various genres, including hip-hop, jazz, funk, and punk rock, with a focus on chaotic, distorted production.
  • Features contributions from artists like Kanye West, Lil Wayne, Schoolboy Q, and Pharrell Williams.
  • Explores individuality, creativity, fame, and Tyler's artistic freedom, reflecting his desire to experiment without industry constraints.
  • Divided critics due to its unconventional style but praised for its ambition and artistic growth.
