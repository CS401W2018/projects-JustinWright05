About IGOR

image reference
  • Released on May 17, 2019.
  • Released under Columbia Records.
  • A conceptual album that narrates the emotional journey of a complicated love triangle.
  • Combines elements of R&B, soul, funk, and hip-hop with experimental production.
  • Includes "EARFQUAKE," "I THINK," and "NEW MAGIC WAND."
  • Features contributions from artists like Kanye West, Playboi Carti, Solange, and CeeLo Green.
  • Won the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album in 2020, though Tyler described it as a genre-defying project.
  • Explores heartbreak, self-reflection, love, and acceptance, presented through the character of Igor.
