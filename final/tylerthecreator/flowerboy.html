Flower Boy

About Flower Boy

  • Released under Columbia Records.
  • Explores self-discovery, loneliness, love, and Tyler's personal growth.
  • Features a mix of lush, melodic instrumentals and introspective lyrics, marking a departure from his earlier aggressive style.
  • Includes contributions from Frank Ocean, Rex Orange County, A$AP Rocky, Kali Uchis, and others.
  • Includes hits like "See You Again," "Boredom," and "911/Mr. Lonely."
  • Widely praised for its vulnerability and production, earning a Grammy nomination for Best Rap Album.
