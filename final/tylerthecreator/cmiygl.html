Call Me If You Get Lost

About Call Me If You Get Lost

  • Released on June 25, 2021.
  • Columbia Records.
  • A travel-themed album that explores themes of self-discovery, love, and personal growth, narrated through the persona of "Tyler Baudelaire."
  • A mix of hip-hop, jazz, soul, and R&B, with a return to rap-heavy production reminiscent of Tyler's earlier work.
  • "WUSYANAME," "LUMBERJACK," and "SWEET / I THOUGHT YOU WANTED TO DANCE."
  • Features artists like Pharrell Williams, Lil Wayne, Brent Faiyaz, and 42 Dugg.
  • Won the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album in 2022.
  • Tackles topics like romantic complications, wealth, identity, and escapism.
  • Included a nostalgic mixtape-style rollout with DJ Drama hosting the album in a Gangsta Grillz-inspired format.
