About Chromakopia

  • released on October 28, 2024, after a brief 12-day promotional period, highlighting Tyler's ability to create anticipation with minimal buildup
  • The album delves deep into Tyler's personal struggles, including his feelings of alienation from fans, relationships, and the pressures of fame. Tracks like “Noid” focus on his discomfort with the parasocial relationships he feels fans expect from him
  • Tyler reflects on his own vulnerabilities, including themes of sexuality, trauma, and parenthood, as seen in songs like “Hey Jane” and “Take Your Mask Off”
  • Chromakopia showcases a more introspective and mature Tyler, with production blending styles from his past works
  • Notable guest features include Lil' Wayne, GloRilla, and Sexyy Red, adding variety to the album's sound
