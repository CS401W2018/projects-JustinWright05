About Goblin

image reference
  • It was released under XL Recordings, an independent British label.
  • The album acts as a continuation of the therapy session narrative started in Tyler's 2009 mixtape Bastard, with a fictional therapist named Dr. TC guiding the storyline.
  • The album faced criticism for its dark, violent, and explicit themes, though Tyler stated much of it was fictional and reflective of his creative mind.
  • Notable tracks include "Yonkers," which gained significant attention for its provocative music video and helped launch Tyler into mainstream recognition.
