Who is Tyler, The Creator?

  • Tyler's album IGOR won the Grammy for Best Rap Album in 2020, marking a significant moment for genre-blending albums that incorporate rap, R&B, and pop influences.
  • Tyler's stage name, "the Creator," originated from a MySpace page he created in his early teens, where he showcased his creative projects, including his own cartoons.
  • Tyler is a fan of animated shows and has lent his voice to various cartoons, including The Jellies! and Regular Show. He co-created The Jellies! with Lionel Boyce.
  • Tyler is passionate about biking. He often talks about how much he loves riding his bike and incorporates bikes into his music videos, such as in the video for "See You Again."
  • In addition to being a rapper, Tyler is also a producer, director, and fashion designer. He co-founded the streetwear brand Golf Wang and often designs the collections himself.
