Wolf

About Wolf

  • It was released under Odd Future Records and RED Distribution.
  • The album is a prequel to Goblin and Bastard, focusing on a fictional camp and Tyler's alter egos, Wolf, Sam, and Dr. TC.
  • Explores Tyler's struggles with fame, relationships, and personal insecurities through a mix of introspective and humorous tracks.
  • Includes fan-favorites like "IFHY," "Domo23," and "Tamale."
  • Praised for its production, with Tyler handling nearly all of it, showcasing growth as an artist and storyteller.
